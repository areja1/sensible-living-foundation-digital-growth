# Automation Architecture

This document outlines automation opportunities for the foundation website.

## Newsletter Flow

Visitor signs up for newsletter  
↓  
Email platform captures contact  
↓  
Welcome email sent  
↓  
Added to monthly newsletter

## Donation Flow

Visitor donates  
↓  
Thank you email sent  
↓  
Impact update email  
↓  
Added to donor list

## Volunteer Flow

Volunteer submits form  
↓  
Confirmation email sent  
↓  
Added to volunteer contact list  
↓  
Volunteer coordinator notified

## Partnership Flow

Partner submits inquiry  
↓  
Thank you email  
↓  
Internal notification sent  
↓  
Partnership pipeline started
